Online Assessment Test Instructions

Important Instructions

Number of Questions: The test consists of 10 multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

Time Limit: You have 30 minutes to complete the test. The timer will start automatically when you begin.

Navigation Buttons:

  • Save & Next: Saves your current response and moves to the next question.
  • Submit: Finalizes your answers and submits the test. You will not be able to modify any answers after submission.

Selection of Options:

  • Each question has four options. Select the correct option by clicking on the radio button next to it.
  • You can change your selection any time before submitting the test.

Clear Response:

  • You can clear your selected answer by clicking the "Clear Response" button for any question. This will leave the question unattempted.

Scoring:

  • 1 mark is awarded for each correct answer.
  • 0 marks for unattempted questions.
  • There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.

Final Score: Your score will be displayed at the end of the test after submission.

Status Indicator:

Question visited but not saved.

Question saved successfully.

Question not yet visited.

Accepting Terms and Conditions:

  • You must accept the terms and conditions by checking the box before starting the exam.
  • Until the checkbox is marked, the Start Exam button will remain inactive and cannot be clicked.
  • Once the terms are accepted, the Start Exam button will activate, allowing you to proceed.

Starting the Exam:

  • Clicking the Start Exam button will begin the test and take you to the questions page.
  • Ensure you are ready before starting, as the timer will begin immediately.
